MAHESH BABU

Mahesh Babu is a highly acclaimed Indian actor and film producer primarily known for his work in the Telugu film industry. He was born on August 9, 1975, in Chennai, India. Mahesh Babu hails from a distinguished film background, with his father, Krishna, being an iconic figure in Telugu cinema. He began his acting career as a child artist in the film "Needa" (1979) and later made his debut as a leading actor with "Raja Kumarudu" (1999). Over the years, he has become a prominent figure in the industry, known for his captivating screen presence and versatile acting abilities. Mahesh Babu has received numerous awards for his performances, including Filmfare Awards and Nandi Awards, which reflect his talent and contribution to Indian cinema. His films span a wide range of genres, showcasing his versatility and ability to portray diverse characters. Aside from acting, Mahesh Babu has ventured into film production through his company G. Mahesh Babu Entertainment Pvt. Ltd. He is not only popular in the South Indian film industry but also has a substantial fan following across India and in various international markets. Beyond his film career, Mahesh Babu is known for his philanthropic endeavors, actively participating in various charitable activities. He is married to Namrata Shirodkar, a former actress and beauty queen, and they have two children.
